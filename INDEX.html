Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most expensive problems a subscription business faces — acquiring a new customer costs 5–7× more than retaining one. This project builds a full analytics pipeline in Excel: from raw customer data through weighted risk scoring, multi-dimensional segmentation, and a management-ready dashboard — without any external tools or code.

Features

CHURN RISK SCORING

Weighted heuristic (0–100) combining inactivity, NPS, support tickets, payment failures, and customer tenure.

KPI DASHBOARD

8 live KPI cards tracking Churn Rate, Retention Rate, At-Risk count, and Average Risk Score — all driven by formulas.

HIGH-RISK WATCHLIST

Auto-filtered view of customers with Risk Score ≥ 40, sorted by severity and flagged CRITICAL or HIGH.

12-MONTH TREND ANALYSIS

Bar and line charts tracking new vs churned customers and monthly churn rate across a full year.

SEGMENTATION

Risk breakdown by Segment (Enterprise / SMB / Startup / Consumer) and Contract Type, with a status pie chart.

ZERO FORMULA ERRORS

All 90 formulas verified and recalculated via LibreOffice. Every cell reference is correct and tested.

Churn Risk Score

risk_score.py
Risk = (Days Inactive / 120) × 30   # inactivity — highest weight
     + (Support Tickets / 15) × 20 # frustration signal
     + (Payment Failures / 5) × 25  # billing risk
     + (Low NPS / 10) × 15      # dissatisfaction
     + (1 − Tenure / 72) × 10  # new customer fragility
Score ≥ 70
🔴 Critical — Churned
Score 40–69
🟠 High Risk — At Risk
Score < 40
🟢 Healthy — Retained

How to Use

Download customer_churn_analysis.xlsx from this repository.

Open in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

Replace the rows in the Customer Data sheet with your own customer records.

All KPIs, charts, risk scores, and the watchlist update automatically via live formulas — no manual recalculation needed.
